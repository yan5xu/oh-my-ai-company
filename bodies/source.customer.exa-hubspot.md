# Exa customer case: HubSpot

S1 official customer case page, fetched 2026-07-08 via `pinixc browser read`.

Facts:
- HubSpot uses Exa to connect Breeze Assistant and Agents to live web intelligence across CRM workflows.
- Quote from Praty Sharma, AI Lead, HubSpot: Exa is necessary for HubSpot agents to supplement internal data with high-quality, real-time people and company search; alternatives were slower, more expensive, and less comprehensive for enrichment coverage.
- HubSpot is described as serving over 299,000 customers across 135 countries.
- Use cases: people and company search, live market signals, funding rounds, leadership changes, product announcements, Monitors API for change events.

Interpretation: HubSpot validates Exa’s enterprise CRM/agent enrichment vertical. This is also a bridge to ABM/GTM intelligence use cases.
