# Paid launch playbook

S1 official Paid blog, fetched 2026-07-08 via `pinixc browser read`.

Launch timeline disclosed:
- March 25, 2025: launch party.
- March 24, 2025: PR and TechCrunch article planned.
- March 20: launch assets finalized.
- March 17: website and brand guidelines completed.
- February 12: begin preparing content.

GTM assets and choices:
- Story: traditional SaaS pricing does not work for AI agent businesses.
- Provocative angle: “The SaaS model is dead and not coming back.”
- Villains: hidden LLM/credit costs, VC pressure for traditional MRR/ARR, outdated seat-based pricing.
- Channels: PR, TechCrunch, founder/investor network, podcasts, launch party, founder posts, manifesto video, week-long content sequence.
- They deprioritized broad press, complex funnels, multi-page site, long-form white papers, and community building before launch.

Reported launch metrics:
- 24h: 15.8K visitors, 47K views, 7,500 first-day visitors, 5,800 second-day visitors.
- LinkedIn: 2,700 new followers, 15,000 impressions.
- Manny announcement: 532,270 impressions, 3,280 reactions, 511 comments, 63 reposts.
- YouTube: 2,202 views, 14.9 hours watched.
- Inbound: >650 signups, >140 scheduled meetings; >1000 signups within a week.
- Launch party: 45 attendees, 20 more on waitlist.

Interpretation: Paid is a strong GTM case because it treats launch as a coordinated campaign, not a one-off announcement. Founder network and investor media support materially changed distribution.
