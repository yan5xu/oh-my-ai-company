# EQT Ventures on Paid seed round

S2 investor-side source, fetched 2026-07-08 via `pinixc browser read`.

Facts:
- EQT says Paid raised a $21M Seed led by Lightspeed, with EQT participating again after leading the pre-seed.
- EQT frames the transition in three waves: seat-based SaaS, usage-based metering, result-based pricing.
- EQT argues usage-based metering is a short-term patch because it often misses full customer value and cost-to-serve of complex agentic workflows.
- Paid is said to enable customers to track costs/margins in real time, adopt results/value-based pricing, and automate billing/ROI reporting.
- Customers named: Artisan, Boon, IFS; claimed 20-40% revenue growth within months, faster sales cycles, stronger retention.
