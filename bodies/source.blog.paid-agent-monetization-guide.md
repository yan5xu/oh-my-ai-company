# Paid guide to AI agent monetization

S1 official Paid blog, fetched 2026-07-08 via `pinixc browser read`.

Framework Paid publishes:
- Agent-based pricing: FTE replacement model.
- Action-based pricing: consumption/usage model.
- Workflow-based pricing: price complete multi-step processes.
- Outcome-based pricing: charge for measured business results.

Important claims:
- Paid says these frameworks come from 60+ AI agent companies.
- Action-based pricing is presented as vulnerable to pricing pressure as AI costs decline.
- Outcome-based pricing is framed as the most future-proof model because it focuses on value delivered, not resources consumed.
- Paid recommends starting simple, then evolving pricing sophistication, while monitoring CAC, LTV, churn by pricing model, usage patterns, and customer feedback.

Interpretation: this is a concept asset as much as a product blog. It gives us vocabulary for evaluating agent companies by monetization maturity, not only by product demo.
