# Paid homepage

S1 official website, fetched 2026-07-08 via `pinixc browser read`.

Key product claims:
- Paid positions itself as the monetization platform for companies building and selling AI agents.
- Core promise: know margins, design pricing, and prove customer value in one place.
- Product modules shown on homepage: Pricing & Credits, Customer Value Receipts, Operator Dashboard, Margin Tracking, Event Monitoring, Billing & Invoicing.
- Pricing modes explicitly supported: credits, usage-based, outcome-based, subscription, and hybrid models.
- The product claims to track every action, task, and tool call and translate those into human-readable signals for cost/value/pricing.

Research interpretation: Paid is not simply billing software. Its wedge is telemetry-to-monetization: observe agent work, attribute cost/value, then turn that into billing, value receipts, and renewal/expansion proof for AI-native companies.
